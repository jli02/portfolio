---the problem---

CUR8 is a startup that curates custom carbon removal portfolios according to customers' needs and goals. Currently, the process of generating a portfolio is done manually, and clients must consult with representatives from CUR8 for several months before making a purchase.

The company is hoping to streamline the purchasing process and has started designing a portfolio simulation tool. They asked us to continue on their design to create a purchasing flow so that users can buy the portfolios that they have generated.

---sketches---

We each designed two potential solutions to generate a wide variety of layout ideas. While we did these independently, we realized that many aspects of our flows overlapped, which helped us decide what would be most intuitive for our first iteration of wireframes.
